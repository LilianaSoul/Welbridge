NIF Registration Service

Standard Service €45
Express Service €67

Delivery: Standard service: 10 business days. Express service: 5-7 business days.

Fiscal Representation: Non-EU residents require a fiscal representative in Portugal. First year of fiscal representation is included. Annual renewal: €67/year.

NISS Registration Service

WelBridge assists with obtaining your Portuguese Social Security Identification Number (NISS), guiding you through the current requirements established by Segurança Social.

NISS Service €189
Processing Time ~8 weeks

Important: Due to requirements implemented in July 2024, NISS applications require proof of employment status and proof of residence permit application. Clients must ensure they meet these requirements before engaging our service.

Visa Application Assistance
