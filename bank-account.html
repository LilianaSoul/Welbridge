Documentation

What You'll Need to Apply

Prepare these documents before starting. We'll review everything and guide you if anything needs adjustment.

Important: Non-EU Nationals

If you hold a passport from outside the European Union and don't currently reside in an EU country, we can only proceed if you can demonstrate that you're in the process of obtaining a Portuguese visa. You'll need to provide proof your visa application is underway, plus supporting documents for your visa category.

Passport Scan

High-quality scanned copy of your valid passport. Phone photos are typically not accepted. Include ID page, signature page, MRZ line, and any pages with stamps/visas.

Proof of Current Address

Utility bill / bank statement / government correspondence dated within the last 3 months, showing your full name and full address.

Proof of Employment or Financial Means

Payslips / contract / employer letter, or for self-employed: tax returns / accounts. For retirees/savings: statements proving sufficient funds.

Portuguese Tax Identification Number (NIF)

A NIF is mandatory for opening any bank account in Portugal. If you don't have a NIF yet, we can help: Apply for your Portuguese NIF.

Tax ID from Your Country (TIN)

If you're tax resident outside Portugal, provide your foreign tax identification number (TIN). The country should match the country of residence listed on your NIF certificate.

For Non-EU Citizens: Proof of Immigration Intent

Proof you are actively pursuing legal residence in Portugal (e.g., consulate/VFS appointment, visa fee receipt, or a visa sticker). Learn more

Language Requirements

Documents must be in English, Spanish, French, or Portuguese. Otherwise, certified translations are required.
