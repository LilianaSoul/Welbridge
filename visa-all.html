Which Visa Is Right for You?

Portugal offers multiple pathways to residency. Each visa is designed for a specific lifestyle and financial situation. Let us help you find your perfect match.

Entrepreneurs

D2 Visa

Build Your Business in Europe

For visionaries ready to launch or expand their business in Portugal. Access the EU market, benefit from competitive tax regimes, and join a thriving startup ecosystem.

  • No minimum investment requirement
  • Viable business plan required
  • Path to citizenship after 5 years
Ideal for: Business owners, investors, freelancers with clients

Passive Income

D7 Visa

Live on Your Own Terms

The golden ticket for retirees and anyone with stable passive income. Enjoy Portugal's sunshine, affordable healthcare, and relaxed lifestyle without employment requirements.

  • Minimum €870/month passive income
  • Access to Portuguese healthcare
  • Citizenship eligibility after 5 years
Ideal for: Retirees, pensioners, rental income earners, investors
Explore D7 Visa
Remote Work

Digital Nomad Visa (D8)

Work Anywhere, Live Here

Portugal's visa for the modern professional. Keep your international clients or remote job while enjoying Lisbon's cafés, Porto's charm, or the Algarve's beaches.

  • Minimum €3,480/month income
  • Work for non-Portuguese companies
  • 2-year renewable residence permit
Ideal for: Remote employees, freelancers, online entrepreneurs
