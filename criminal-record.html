About This Document

What Is a Portuguese Criminal Record Certificate?

The Certificado de Registo Criminal is an official document issued by the Portuguese Ministry of Justice. It confirms whether you have any criminal convictions registered in Portugal's national database.

This certificate is essential for various legal, professional, and immigration purposes — both within Portugal and internationally.

  • Issued by the Ministry of Justice
  • Valid for international use
  • Digitally verifiable and secure
  • Available in Portuguese (translation available)
Who Needs This?

Common Reasons to Request a Certificate

This document is frequently required for official, professional, and personal purposes.

Visa Applications

Required for most residence visa categories including D7, D2, and family reunification visas.

Employment

Many employers request this for background checks, especially in education, healthcare, and finance.

Citizenship

Essential for Portuguese nationality applications and dual citizenship processes.

Adoption

Required documentation for national and international adoption proceedings.
