The D7 visa requires proof of regular passive income (minimum €760/month), valid passport, criminal record certificate, health insurance, proof of accommodation, and a Portuguese NIF. We provide a complete checklist and guide you through every step.

Non-EU residents who obtain a Portuguese NIF are legally required to have a fiscal representative. This person or company receives tax correspondence on your behalf and ensures you stay compliant with Portuguese tax authorities.

We offer a money-back guarantee on most services. If your application is rejected due to our error, you'll receive a full refund. For rejections due to factors outside our control, we'll work with you to resolve the issue or provide a partial refund.

The D7 visa is for individuals with passive income (pensions, investments, rental income). The Digital Nomad visa is for remote workers employed by companies outside Portugal or self-employed with international clients. Both lead to residency, but have different income requirements.
