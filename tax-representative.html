The Challenge

Digital Notifications Aren't Always Enough

Portuguese law allows electronic notifications, but certain official communications are still exclusively sent by physical mail.

What Could Be Waiting in Your Mailbox?

If your registered tax address is outside Portugal, critical letters from Finanças may never reach you. By the time you discover a problem, deadlines may have passed and penalties may have accumulated.

  • Audit & Documentation Requests Tax authorities requesting additional information or clarification
  • Fines & Payment Warnings Notifications of penalties, late fees, or overdue tax obligations
  • Legal Summons & Enforcement Formal legal notices and debt collection communications

The Solution: A Local Representative

With a licensed tax representative in Portugal, every piece of correspondence from Finanças is received at a local address, scanned, and delivered to your email within hours.

  • Physical mail converted to digital instantly
  • Never miss a deadline again
  • Peace of mind for €67/year
