Why It Matters

A Document Without Proper Certification Is Just Paper

You've gathered all your important documents — birth certificates, diplomas, contracts, powers of attorney. But without proper certification and authentication, Portuguese authorities will simply reject them.

For document copies to have legal validity in Portugal, they must be certified and authenticated. Government entities, banks, and institutions require proof that your documents are genuine reproductions of the originals.

"We've seen visa applications denied, citizenship processes delayed, and bank account openings rejected — all because of missing certifications. Don't let paperwork be the obstacle to your goals."
